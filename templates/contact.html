FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer?

Visit our Volunteer page and fill out the registration form. Our team will reach out with next steps within 48 hours.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes! NayePankh Foundation is 80G certified. All donations are eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

Can I start a chapter in my city?

Absolutely! We're always looking for passionate individuals to lead local chapters. Contact us to learn about our chapter leader program.

How is my donation used?

We maintain 100% transparency. Every donation directly funds our programs — education supplies, meals, hygiene kits, and animal care. We publish regular impact reports.
