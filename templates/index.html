Who We Are

Empowering Communities,
Transforming Lives

Born during the COVID-19 pandemic in 2021 by a group of passionate high school students, NayePankh Foundation has grown into one of India's largest youth-led nonprofit organizations, touching over 2,00,000 lives across 50+ cities.

Our Initiatives

Programs That Create
Real Impact

Every initiative is designed to address the root causes of inequality and provide sustainable solutions for underprivileged communities.

Education

Shiksha for All

Providing holistic education, school supplies, and mentorship to underprivileged children, ensuring every child gets a chance to learn and grow.

Nutrition

Annapurna Drive

Regular food distribution drives to ensure no one goes to bed hungry. We've served over 1,00,000 meals to those in need across India.

Health

Swasthya Initiative
