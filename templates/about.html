NayePankh Foundation was born during the COVID-19 pandemic when a group of high school students witnessed the devastating impact on underprivileged communities. What started as a small relief effort has grown into a nationwide movement of young changemakers.

🎯

Our Mission

To uplift underprivileged communities by providing education, basic necessities, and healthcare access while fostering a culture of compassion.

🌟

Our Vision

An India where every individual has access to quality education, nutrition, healthcare, and lives with dignity regardless of their background.

💎

Our Values

Compassion, Transparency, Youth Empowerment, Inclusivity, and Sustainable Impact drive everything we do.

Our Journey

Milestones Along
The Way

2021 — The Beginning

Founded During COVID-19

A group of high school students started distributing food and essentials to families affected by the pandemic in their neighborhood.

2022 — Growing Roots

Registered as a Society

Officially registered under the Societies Registration Act. Obtained 12A and 80G certifications. Expanded to 10+ cities.

2023 — Scaling Impact

1,00,000 Lives Touched

Launched the Shiksha for All education program. Started menstrual hygiene awareness campaigns across rural India.

2024 — National Reach

Pan-India Movement

Reached 50+ cities with active volunteer chapters. Crossed 2,00,000 lives impacted. Became one of India's largest youth-led NGOs.

2025 — The Future

Building Sustainable Change

Focus on long-term community development programs, digital literacy, and creating self-sustaining community centers.
